Introduction

Dinara Kelyamova is a graphic designer and artist with over a decade of hands-on experience in Lviv, Ukraine, Wrocław, Poland and Montréal, QC.

Her graphic design work aims to help businesses maximize their full potential using visual communication to reach their audiences and develop brand identity. Dinara is proficient in user experience and interface, branding design and marketing content.

As an artist, Dinara explores various themes of the human experience through urban paintings, sketches, drawings, etchings and photography. Her work was exhibited in Lviv, Ukraine and Wrocław, Poland as well as the creation of the clothing brand KELYAM.
