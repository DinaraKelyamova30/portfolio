Introduction

Hi. My name is Dinara. I am a graduate (Bachelor of Graphic Design) of the Lviv National Academy of Art and have over a decade of hands-on experience as a Designer and Artist.

I use the power of design to help businesses and entrepreneurs succeed at building their dreams. I believe that through the design we can make life better, more beautiful, solving business problems and understanding humanity’s needs.
